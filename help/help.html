Clip It! - Extension Help

Click the extension icon to open the main interface. From there, you can:

  • 📋Clip the coupons
  • 🤑Clip only the FREE coupons
  • 🖇️ Unclip all the coupons

Here are some of the features of the extension:

  • ✨Number of coupons to be clipped customization
  • ✨Free coupons clipping
  • ✨Clip and unclip coupons

If you encounter any issues, try the following:

  1. 🔃 Refresh the page
  2. ✅ Disable and re-enable the extension
  3. 🗑️ Clear your browser cache

Configure the maximum number of coupons to be clipped to lower number for better performance e.g. 20 or 30.
